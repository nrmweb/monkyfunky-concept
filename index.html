Our Story

Vibrant Food for
Every Body

Monky Funky Café was built on one idea — that vegetarian food should be bold, fun, and never boring. We opened on Granville Street in Halifax to bring colourful bubble teas, freshly made matcha, and a fully plant-based menu to the city.

Seven days a week, open late on weekends — the café is always your place.
